Session: Combination Passing Pattern

 

Screen 1

Simple combination passing pattern to develop player final third passing and switching play ability

player pass according to passing arrow and follow their passes.

Progression:

give and go on the final pass before ball heading to the start

4 balls going at the same time

 

Screen 2

1. players dribble to 1st cone and pass back to start, as this happened both player who pass back make a diagonal run to receive another pass and pass it back on touch passing and follow pass. 2nd passer sprint to the back of the start line.

2. technical passing side volley, instep volley, header and player receive 2nd ball and dribble to far cone.

3. two players combine passes to each other until they reach 1st cone, as this happened the last play to receive a pass take the ball back and begin a new combination passes while the 1st player move on to receive 2nd pass and play a give n go with that player.

 

Screen 3

Final third Passing combination with attacking progression to goal. Player with ball paly a pass in the wide area and move into the attacking space in the central area to receive the ball and attack space in the central area before play a pass attacking player

 

Screen 4

combination playing with switching of play from the central area to the wide area with overlapping run in the wide area and reverse recovery run to take up position.

Instruction:

A - B -C - D - wide area - B who overlap and pass to E -D - F - wide area - D who overlap to pass to A.

Rotation:

A replace B, B replace C, C replace A, E replace D, D replace F, F replace E

 

Screen 5

Set up A pass to B, and move toward B receive a pass and switch to C, A replace B, B replace C, C pass to A, and move toward A to receive a pass back from A and play back to A who then switch to D, and move toward D to receive a pass back to and switch to C. Squence continue

 

Screen 6

Set Up: ball start at Station 4 pass to 1 and follow, 1 pass to 2 and over lap to recive the ball in the wide area from middle player, and pass to 3 and follow 2 move to 4, 3 pass to 4 and overlap, to receive the pass in the wide area from the middle player and pass to 1, after the pass to middle player by 4, 4 move to 2. Squence continue

 

Screen 7

Set up: two balls start together A pass to B - C - E - F, F dribble to A and pass immediately to B
